# Mapping Opportunity Access for Students and Job Seekers in the DMV Region

An ArcGISPro project scoring and mapping the access that students and young job seekers have to professional activities in the DC metro area.

This project aims to investigate which areas in the DMV region (DC-Maryland-Virginia Metropolitan area provide the best overall access to opportunity for students and job seekers. 
Specifically, it evaluates how access to employment hubs, proximity to transit, and affordability interact to shape where young people can realistically live and access professional opportunities. 
The analysis' scope focuses on census tract-level variation within the DMV and produces a composite "opportunity accessibility score" to highlight the most favorable areas for young professionals.
